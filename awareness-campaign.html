Awareness Campaign

“We don’t just deliver freight; we also deliver awareness.”
Jim Barrett, President & CEO

Next

It all started with a bright pink tractor trailer.

One Saturday, Jim Barrett, President and CEO, was unable to attend a walk in support of Breast Cancer Awareness with his family. Sensing their disappointment, Jim promised to do something “big” to make up for missing the event.

A week later, a beautiful, bright pink trailer rolled on to the Road Scholar lot. That was the birth of a program – in partnership with customers and organizations – that has helped to raise awareness for dozens of affiliations, groups, and causes, using the company’s tractor trailers as “rolling billboards.”

Today, Road Scholar Transport has over 40 pieces of equipment in support of charities and causes, and each awareness trailer that is on the road receives between 30,000 – 80,000 impressions in a single day!

Our awareness trailers have also become a sought-after backdrop for special events, and we always do our best to accommodate and help each cause!
