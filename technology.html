Technology

Road Scholar Technology

Trucking technology has revolutionized the industry,
allowing us to pinpoint the location of a carrier
at any moment and making it possible to maintain the integrity of
temperature-protected products.

Next
  • All Road Scholar Transport trailers are equipped with Samsara road and driver-facing in-cab cameras
  • Samsara satellite tracking tracks our assets and your product, whether or not they are hooked to a tractor.
  • Orbcomm satellite tracking platform allow us to monitor and change temperature remotely and provides back-up to Samsara satellites.
  • Reefer temperatures are monitored both manually and on an excursion-based protocol by the Road Scholar Temp Team the entire time the product is in our possession.
  • ERP system is integrated with Orbcomm, if actual reefer temperatures are out of the acceptable range, our Temp Team is alerted, and action is taken immediately.
  • No matter the time of day or night, someone from the Road Scholar Temp Team has their eye on the gauge!
