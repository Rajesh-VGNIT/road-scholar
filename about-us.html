About Us

At Road Scholar Transport, we don’t strive to be good, we strive to be remarkable.
The bedrock for remarkable results are remarkable people, and our culture is
the underpinning of our remarkable team.

Next

Road Scholar Transport is a large asset-based carrier serving the Truckload, LTL and specialty service needs of businesses in the northeast and Mid-Atlantic US. Our customers include some of the top names in the pharmaceutical and food and beverage industries, the Department of Defense, as well as a range of businesses focused on customized shipping solutions and high quality services.

Founded in 1988 by Jim and Debra Barrett, Road Scholar Transport has grown from one owner-operator to a fleet of over 105 tractors, 300 trailers and 65 refrigerated units. While our technology and methods have evolved over the years, our core values and beliefs remain the same.

Our purpose is to provide 1st class transportation products and solutions while creating extraordinary customer experiences.

Our first responsibility is to our customer – as individuals, their product, and their brand. We believe in always doing what’s best for the customer, even if it’s to our own detriment. We approach every customer–and the customer’s customer – with the same dignity, respect and professionalism we would afford our own family.

We are committed to providing our customers with innovative tracking and security technology solutions at a fair return. We are committed to safety at our facilities and on roadways. We are committed to protecting the environment and to energy efficiency.

We believe in doing the right thing – always
